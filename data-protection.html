Data Protection

At Krushing Services, we are committed to protecting your personal data and complying with data protection laws. This policy outlines how we handle and safeguard the information you provide to us.

Data We Collect

We may collect personal data such as your name, contact information, and any other information you choose to provide. We collect this data when you contact us, use our services, or visit our website.

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used to provide you with our services, improve our website, and communicate with you. We will never sell your data to third parties and will only share it when legally required or to fulfill our contractual obligations.

Your Rights

  • Access: You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal limitations.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Protection Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please check back periodically for updates.
